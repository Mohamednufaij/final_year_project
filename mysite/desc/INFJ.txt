INFJ (Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging):
INFJs are insightful and empathetic individuals who possess a deep understanding of human emotions and motivations. They are driven by their values and have a strong desire to make a positive impact on the world. INFJs are intuitive and often trust their gut instincts when making decisions. They are adept at seeing the bigger picture and are skilled at recognizing patterns and possibilities. INFJs are compassionate listeners and are known for their ability to connect with others on a deep and meaningful level.
