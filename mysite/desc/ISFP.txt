ISFP (Introverted, Sensing, Feeling, Perceiving):
ISFPs are gentle and caring individuals who appreciate beauty and aesthetics in their surroundings. They have a strong appreciation for art, nature, and sensory experiences. ISFPs are compassionate and value harmony in their relationships. They are often creative and have a talent for expressing themselves through various artistic mediums. ISFPs are flexible and adaptable, and they often approach life with a laid-back and easygoing attitude.
