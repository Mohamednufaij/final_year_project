INTJ (Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging):
INTJs are strategic and independent thinkers who thrive in complex and challenging situations. They have a natural aptitude for analyzing information and developing innovative solutions. INTJs are highly logical and rational, and they excel at conceptualizing abstract ideas. They are focused and determined individuals who set high standards for themselves and work tirelessly to achieve their goals. INTJs value efficiency and effectiveness, and they often possess a strong sense of self-confidence.
