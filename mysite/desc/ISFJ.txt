ISFJ (Introverted, Sensing, Feeling, Judging):
ISFJs are compassionate and nurturing individuals who are deeply in tune with the needs of others. They have a strong sense of duty and are dedicated to helping and supporting those around them. ISFJs excel at creating a harmonious and stable environment, often assuming caregiving roles. They are attentive listeners and value maintaining strong personal relationships. ISFJs are reliable, responsible, and often go above and beyond to ensure the well-being of others.
