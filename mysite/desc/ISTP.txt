ISTP (Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Perceiving):
ISTPs are adventurous and resourceful individuals who enjoy hands-on problem-solving and exploration. They have a strong ability to analyze and understand how things work, often excelling in technical or mechanical fields. ISTPs are independent and enjoy autonomy in their work. They are adaptable and quick to respond to changing circumstances. ISTPs are known for their calm and collected demeanor, as well as their ability to remain composed under pressure.
