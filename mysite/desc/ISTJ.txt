ISTJ (Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Judging):
ISTJs are practical and detail-oriented individuals who prefer to work systematically and follow established rules and procedures. They have a strong sense of responsibility and take their commitments seriously. They are reliable, organized, and excel at tasks that require attention to detail. ISTJs are known for their logical and analytical thinking, making them excellent problem solvers. They value stability, order, and predictability, and are often seen as dependable and trustworthy individuals.
